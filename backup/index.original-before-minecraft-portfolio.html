I'm a Minecraft Bedrock developer with a focus on scripted gameplay, custom entities, and boss encounters. My background is in game design and web development — I bring that same engineering discipline to the Bedrock ecosystem, treating every add-on like a proper software project.

I work with TypeScript, the Script API, molang, and deep JSON structures to build things that feel polished, perform well on low-end devices, and surprise players. I've shipped on the Marketplace, collaborated with server networks, and led small teams building ambitious content.

Outside of code, I'm obsessed with the intersection of constraint and creativity — the idea that a grid of cubes can hold an entire universe is still, to me, the best design brief ever written.

02 — Featured Work

The Ender Dragon Remastered

Live on Marketplace
Phase 3 — Crystal Resurrection Sequence
Gameplay Capture · 4K · 2:14
01 / 04

A full reimagining of Minecraft's iconic end-game fight.

A complete overhaul of the Ender Dragon encounter with three phases, scripted crystal sequences, custom attack patterns, and a choreographed outro. Built entirely with the Script API and custom JSON entities to stay fully Bedrock-native — no mods, no workarounds.

Full Case Study
  • Three distinct combat phases Each with unique attack sets, mobility patterns, and player-response windows.
  • Scripted crystal mechanic Crystals regenerate on a weighted schedule, forcing positional decisions in real time.
  • Custom particle & sound design Over 40 new particles and a full layered audio mix built from the ground up.
  • Optimized for mobile devices Holds 60 FPS on mid-range Android during peak combat load — tested extensively.
  • Localized in 8 languages Full dialogue and UI translations shipped at launch.
